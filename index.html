About

Our vision is to initiate a resource for college students to track the amount of caffeine they consume and help them reduce unhealthy use of the stimulant.

We hope to provide a website that can achieve this by reminding students of the harmful effects of a dependence on the stimulant, and providing a useful resource to log intake to calculate the approximate halflife of caffeine in their system.
